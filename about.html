about

I’m Akasa Mei, a media artist and designer based between Los Angeles and Shanghai. My work moves across interactive media, game design, installation, creative coding, video, and visual storytelling.

Studying Media Arts and Practice at USC, I often work with tools such as p5.js, Processing, Unreal Engine, Rhino, Blender, Adobe software, and video editing. I like building projects that feel atmospheric, emotionally layered, and visually immersive.

Moving between LA and Shanghai has shaped the way I think about identity, language, culture, and space. This in-between experience often appears in my work through layered images, fictional worlds, shifting environments, and stories about transformation.

I also have experience in event production, stage management, design support, and collaborative project leadership. I enjoy creating work that is both conceptually meaningful and carefully built.
